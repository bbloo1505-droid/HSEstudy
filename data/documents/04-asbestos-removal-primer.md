# Study primer: Asbestos removal and clearance

[[page:rem-1]]

## Licensing snapshot (learn the structure)

- **Friable removal** — Class A licensed removalist.
- **Non-friable removal above 10 m²** — appropriately licensed business.
- Smaller non-friable quantities still require competent, controlled work and may have notification/requirements — verify in current law/code.

## Air monitoring and clearance

[[page:rem-2]]

Air monitoring may be used during or after removal to help assess control effectiveness. **Clearance inspections** confirm the removal area is visually clean and suitable for reoccupation according to the applicable process. Clearance is not a general statement that “the building is safe forever.”

## Chain of custody and laboratory analysis

[[page:rem-3]]

Samples must be uniquely identified, sealed, labelled and transferred with a chain-of-custody record. Laboratories report against the method used. Reconcile lab IDs to field IDs before drafting findings. If reconciliation fails, do not invent results.

## Professional boundaries

[[page:rem-4]]

Do not approve removal methodologies beyond your competence and authorisation. Do not declare ACM from photos. Escalate licensing, clearance and interpretation questions to your supervisor.
